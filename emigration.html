ECR vs ECNR: What You Need to Know

Understanding the difference between ECR and ECNR status

ECR

Emigration Check Required

ECR status applies to Indian passport holders who have not completed 10th standard (SSC) education. This means you need to obtain clearance from the Protector of Emigrants (POE) before traveling to any of the 18 ECR countries for employment.

Who falls under ECR?
  • Individuals who have not passed 10th standard
  • Individuals with ECR stamping on their passport
  • Individuals traveling to specified 18 countries for employment
ECNR

Emigration Check Not Required

ECNR status is granted to Indian passport holders who have completed at least 10th standard education. With ECNR status, you do not need clearance from the POE for traveling abroad for employment.

Who falls under ECNR?
  • Graduates and professionals
  • Individuals who have passed at least 10th standard
  • Individuals with ECNR stamping on passport
  • Persons holding diplomatic/official passports

Employment Documents
